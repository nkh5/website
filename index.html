About me

Hi — I'm Nicholas, a rising senior at Rice. The question I keep coming back to is the one every chip team has to answer: how do you know it works? I like building the machinery that answers it — golden models and scoreboards before silicon exists, automated benches once it does.

Before this I tested industrial drives at Rockwell Automation, and these days I split my time between ML research with Baylor College of Medicine and leading hardware integration for Rice's OwlSat CubeSat. Off the clock: I'm probably reworking a PCB I already called done.
